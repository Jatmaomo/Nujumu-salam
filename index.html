About The Modirosah

History & Vision

Founded in the vibrant community of Yemetu Aladorin, Ibadan, NUJUMU SALAM AROBIYAH TUL ISLAMIYAH was established with a clear mission: to provide authentic and holistic Islamic education. We are committed to blending the study of classical Arabic texts with modern, effective teaching methods, ensuring our students receive a comprehensive foundation.

Our vision is to cultivate students who are proficient in the Noble Qur'an, fluent in the Arabic language, and grounded in the exemplary Islamic character (Akhlaq), preparing them to be morally upright leaders in their communities and beyond.

Administration & Location

Our Programs & Courses

القرآن الكريم

Al-Qur’an Kareem & Tajweed

Comprehensive sessions focusing on correct recitation (Tajweed) and various levels of memorization (Hifz) of the Noble Qur'an. Suitable for all ages.

  • Qur’an memorization (Hifz)
  • Applied Tajweed rules
اللغة العربية

Arabic Language (Lughatul Arabiyah)

From absolute beginner level to advanced mastery. Students learn to read, write, speak, and understand classical and modern Arabic.

  • Arabic Grammar (Nahw & Sarf)
  • Classical Arabic texts (Ajrumiyyah, Balagha)
تربية إسلامية

Islamic Studies (Tarbiyyah Islamiyyah)

Essential Fiqh (jurisprudence), Aqidah (creed), Seerah (prophetic biography), and the cultivation of proper Islamic morals and etiquette.

  • Fiqh & Aqidah
  • Islamic morals and etiquette

Class Modalities

We offer flexible learning options to suit every schedule: Weekend Classes | Weekday Classes | Online Learning | Physical Attendance
